# Project Setu (Nayka Community Portal) - Admin Panel Report

This report outlines the current state and capabilities of the Nayka Admin Panel, detailing the centralized Content Management System (CMS) features available to administrators and moderators for managing the Project Setu portal.

**Note:** Administrators have full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operation capabilities for each of the sections outlined below.

---

## 📊 1. Dashboard Overview (`/admin`)
The central hub providing administrators with a quick glance at the platform's health and activity.
- **Key Metrics:** Total Members, Pending Verifications, Published Announcements.
- **Recent Activity:** Tracks admin actions and platform events.

## 👥 2. User & Access Management (`/admin/users`)
Tools for maintaining the security and integrity of the Private Member Hub.
- **User Approvals:** Review and verify new member registrations.
- **Role Management:** Manage `GUEST`, `MEMBER`, `VERIFIED_MEMBER`, `MODERATOR`, and `ADMIN` roles.

## 🏛️ 3. Heritage Dashboard (`/admin/heritage`)
Museum-grade Heritage Archive Manager for preserving the Nayka culture.
- **Add New Heritage Item:** Manage Stories, Photos, Videos, and Audio.
- **Manage Galleries:** Organize visual and media content into curated galleries.
- **Manage TK Labels:** Administer Traditional Knowledge (TK) labels for culturally sensitive items.
- **Oral History Submissions:** Review and manage community submissions with an approval queue.
- **Reports:** Generate and view Heritage Content Inventory reports.

## 📰 4. News & Updates (`/admin/news`)
Centralized management of official community communications and updates.
- **Add New Post/Announcement:** Create and publish articles and breaking news.
- **Manage Announcement Ticker:** Control scrolling alerts and urgent notices on the portal.
- **Fact Check Posts:** Publish official verifications to counter misinformation.
- **Official Statements:** Issue formal communications from the community leadership.
- **Newsletter:** Create, design, and send community newsletters.
- **Subscriber Management:** Manage the mailing list and newsletter subscribers.

## 📁 5. Resources Manager (`/admin/resources`)
Comprehensive board manager for community welfare and development opportunities.
- **Schemes:** Add, Edit, and Delete Central, State, and NGO welfare schemes.
- **Scheme Finder Configuration:** Customize logic and criteria for the public scheme discovery tool.
- **Jobs Board:** Add, Edit, and Delete public job opportunities (with auto-expire functionality).
- **Scholarships:** Add, Edit, and Delete educational funding opportunities.
- **Trainings:** Add, Edit, and Delete skill development programs.
- **Forms Library:** Upload, categorize, and manage downloadable official forms.

## 🛍️ 6. Adi Haat (Artisan Hub) (`/admin/adi-haat`)
Moderation and management of the community marketplace.
- **Artisans:** Add, Edit, and Approve artisan profiles and verifications.
- **Products:** Add and Edit product listings (automatically linked to verified Artisans).
- **Inquiries Log:** Track and manage customer inquiries and communications.
- **Artisan Success Stories:** Publish features and case studies highlighting artisan achievements.

## 💍 7. Matrimony Moderation (`/admin/matrimony`)
- **Profile Approvals:** Tools to review and explicitly approve prospective matrimony profiles before they are visible to the community.

## 📬 8. Operations & Support
- **Media Archive (`/admin/media`):** Centralized repository for managing platform images and documents.
- **Contact Inbox (`/admin/contact`):** A unified inbox to read and resolve messages, inquiries, and support requests.

---

*Note: The Admin Panel provides full, granular control over all public and secure sections of the community portal.*
