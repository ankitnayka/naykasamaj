# Project Setu (Nayka Community Portal) - Features & Services Report

This report outlines the current state of the Project Setu portal, detailing all fully implemented features and active services available to the community.

---

## 🏛️ Section A: Public Portal Features
*(Visible to everyone - for awareness, outreach, and public utility)*

### 1. Digital Heritage and Culture Hub (`/heritage`)
A dedicated space to preserve and celebrate the Nayka community's rich history.
- **Interactive Storytelling Timelines:** Documenting Nayaka origins from antiquity to the present day.
- **Multimedia Archives:** 
  - **Photo Gallery:** Curated community photos sorted by events and festivals.
  - **Video Gallery:** Archival and recent footage of ceremonies and celebrations.
  - **Audio & Oral History:** Recordings of traditional songs and elder narratives.
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Implementation of Traditional Knowledge (TK) labels to warn users before accessing culturally sensitive content.

### 2. Official Information and News (`/news`)
The central source of truth for community updates and announcements.
- **Announcement Ticker:** Real-time scrolling updates visible across the portal.
- **News Categories:**
  - **Breaking News Alerts:** Urgent updates and announcements.
  - **Fact Check Corner:** A dedicated section to debunk rumors and combat misinformation.
  - **Official Statements:** Archive of messages from community leaders.
  - **Achievements Blog:** Highlighting community successes and milestones.

### 3. Public Resources and Opportunities (`/schemes` & `/jobs`)
Tools to connect members with government and community assistance.
- **Interactive Scheme Finder:** A GEET-style filterable directory of Central, State, and NGO welfare schemes.
- **Scholarship Alerts:** High-visibility banners warning users of upcoming application deadlines.
- **Downloadable Forms:** Direct links to download official application PDF forms for eligible schemes.
- **Job & Skill Board:** 
  - Listings for public job opportunities.
  - Directory of government and private skill development programs.

### 4. Adi Haat Community Marketplace (`/adi-haat`)
A digital marketplace to support local Nayka artisans and businesses.
- **Product Showcase:** A visual gallery of products categorized by Handicrafts, Textiles, Food, and Art.
- **Artisan Directory:** Dedicated profiles for artisans detailing their craft, bio, location, and direct contact numbers.
- **Success Stories:** Featured spotlights highlighting how the platform has aided individual artisans.
- **Direct Inquiry:** Price listings coupled with buttons to directly coordinate with sellers.

---

## 🔒 Section B: Private Member Hub Features
*(Secure login required - exclusively for verified Nayaka community members)*

### 1. Member Directory and Networking (`/members`)
A secure space to find and connect with verified community members globally.
- **Secure Registration & Approval:** All new registrations are placed in a waiting queue until a platform Admin verifies their identity.
- **Search & Filter:** A robust database searchable by Name, and filterable by exact Village/City and Profession.
- **Detailed Profiles:** Member cards display the user's Profile Avatar, explicit Community Role (Admin, Moderator, Member), Location, Profession, and a personal Bio.

### 2. Community Forum and Help Board (`/forum`)
A safe, internal discussion board for community matters.
- **Categorized Discussions:** Topic-based threads for Education, Farming, Health, Business, and Culture.
- **Emergency Assistance:** Highlighted emergency request board for urgent help coordination.
- **Post Interactions:** Support for liking and commenting on community posts.
- **Privacy Controls:** An option to post anonymously for sensitive topics or questions.

### 3. Matrimony Corner (`/matrimony`)
A secure matchmaking tool strictly limited to verified members for privacy.
- **Profile Browsing:** View detailed prospective profiles including age, education, occupation, and location.
- **Interest Expression:** Secure buttons to express interest in specific profiles.

### 4. Real-Time Engagement (`/events`)
- **Dynamic Event Calendar:** A comprehensive calendar tracking upcoming community meetings, cultural festivals, health drives, and educational seminars.

---

## ⚙️ Section C: Technical and Admin Features
*(Backend infrastructure for managers)*

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Distinct permissions and views for `GUEST`, `MEMBER`, `VERIFIED_MEMBER`, `MODERATOR`, and `ADMIN`.
- **Admin Dashboard:** High-level metrics tracking total registered members, pending verification requests, and total active announcements.
- **Verification Management:** Tools allowing administrators to approve new platform registrations to ensure a safe, closed community environment.

---

*Note: The platform is currently operating in Phase 1. Features designated for Phase 2 (Mobile Apps, E-Learning Hub, Telemedicine, and advanced digital payment integrations) are scheduled for future development cycles.*
